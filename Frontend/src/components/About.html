What is Whiskr

Pet health management dashboard to keep reminders of vet appointments, track medications, hold medical records in one easy to access dashboard.
Extra handy features: AI assistance, search for nearby veterinarians and groomers,
and get tips of breed-specific care for your fuzzy friends!
